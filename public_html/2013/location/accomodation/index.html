UJI's Student Residence

Geomundus 2013 has selected the UJI's student residence as the best place to accommodate in Castelló during the Conference days, it is located within the campus of the University Jaume I (UJI), 2.5 km from the city center and about 5-10 minutes walk from any faculty. The residence is well connected to the city center by bus (lines 3, 10, 11 and 12) and Tram (L T-1). More information here.

Campus del Riu Sec: +34 964 730 830

If you are interested in this option, please click here and fill the form.

Each room has:

  • Bed.
  • Bathroom with shower.
  • Wardrobe.
  • Desk and chair.
  • Kitchen with electric hob plate.
  • Microwave.
  • Sink.
  • Refrigerator and Warmer.
  • Air conditioning / heating.
  • Telephone.
  • Internet connection and TV point.


All exterior with large bay windows.
