Parking

In front of the castle, on the Hindenburg place a sufficient number of pay parking for your car. If you stay in the city, we recommend the use of well-developed mass transit system or bicycles. By car you arrive at the short distances, often significantly slower. An overview of the route network of the local transport you can find here … At various points you can also rent bicycles, more information can be found at the Tourist Office: http://www.muenster.de/stadt/tourismus/radverleih.html
