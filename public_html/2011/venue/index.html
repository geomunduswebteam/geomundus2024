Venue

Conference Venue:

The conference will take place at the Münster Castle (Schloss Münster), Schlossplatz 2, 48149 Münster. This building, built between 1767 – 1787 to 1803 and seat of the bishop of Münster, located since its destruction during the Second World War and subsequent reconstruction of the head office of the  Westfälische Wilhelms-Universität Münster.
