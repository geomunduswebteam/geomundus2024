Call for Short Paper and Abstract

Students who are currently pursuing undergraduate, graduate, and post-graduate studies are encouraged to submit their original research work as a 2-4 pages extended short paper or/and an abstract of 300-350 words for poster. This conference offers an excellent platform to share your research findings through paper and poster presentations and exchange knowledge with a broad audience.


Prize for the Best Paper: The conference organizing team is pleased to announce that travel support will be given to the two best papers.
Short Paper and Abstract Submission Deadline: Estimated August 2024
Notification of Short Paper and Abstract Acceptance: Estimated September 2024


You are invited to submit your research work based on the following two topics:

1. Focus Topic:

  • Environmental monitoring and modeling
  • Food Security and Sustainable Agriculture
  • Climate Resilience and Disaster Management
  • Land Tenure Security and Land Economy
  • Conflict management
  • Urban Management, Smart Cities, and Renewable Energy
  • Ecosystem Management and Biodiversity Conservation
  • Geo-health

2. General Topic of Geoinformatics, and Geospatial Technologies (But not limited to):

  • Cadastre
  • Cartography
  • Environmental Management
  • Epidemiology
  • GIS Software Engineering
  • Geodesy
  • Geographic Information Science
  • Geography
  • Geoinformatics
  • Geospatial Databases
  • Geospatial Data Mining
  • Geostatistics
  • Landscape Ecology
  • Location Based Services
  • Navigation
  • Remote Sensing Applications
  • Spatial Cognition
  • Spatial Data Science
  • Surveying
  • Unmanned Aerial Systems/LIDAR
  • Others

Short papers accepted and presented at the conference will be published on the GeoMundus Website.

Paper submission: Authors are requested to submit their short paper (2-4 pages excluding references) according to the template set by the organizing committee.

Abstract submission for poster: Authors are requested to submit their abstracts within 300-350 words who are willing to present their work by poster.

Presentation: Short paper and poster will be presented in different sessions.

  • All short papers will be presented in the scientific paper presentation session. It consists of 10 minutes of presentation and 5 minutes of discussion.
  • All posters will be presented in a poster session on the first day of the conference.
